Everything you need to know about our platform, services, and how to get started.

Courses & Education

Do I need prior experience to join?
No experience is required. We have courses specifically designed for complete beginners, starting from what Forex is and how currency markets work. Our learning path guides you step by step from absolute beginner to advanced trader.
Do I get lifetime access to courses?
Yes. Once you purchase a course or subscribe to a paid plan, you get lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates. You can revisit any lesson as many times as you need.
Are the courses available on mobile?
Yes. Our student portal is fully responsive and works on all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. You can learn on the go from anywhere in the world.
How long does it take to complete a course?
It depends on the course and your pace. Most courses range from 6–24 hours of video content. Since they're self-paced, you can take as long as you need. We recommend dedicating at least 1–2 hours per day for best results.

Trading Signals

How are your trading signals delivered?
Signals are delivered via our private Telegram channel, WhatsApp group, and the student portal. You receive the currency pair, entry price, stop loss, take profit levels, and a brief analysis for each signal.
What is your average signal win rate?
Our average win rate over the past 24 months is 79.4%. All results are publicly documented in our signal history log, which you can access in the student portal. We never hide losing trades.
How many signals do you send per week?
We typically send 8–15 signals per week across Forex majors, crosses, and Synthetic Indices. Quality always comes before quantity — we only send signals with high-probability setups.

Account Management

How does account management work?
You fund a trading account in your own name on your preferred broker. Our verified traders are given limited power of attorney to trade on your behalf. You retain full ownership and can withdraw at any time. Monthly profit splits are clearly defined before you join.
What is the minimum capital for account management?
Our Starter account management plan requires a minimum of $500. For the best returns, we recommend at least $2,500 (Growth plan) where our traders have more flexibility with position sizing.
Is my capital guaranteed?
No capital in trading is ever 100% guaranteed. Trading involves risk. However, we implement strict drawdown limits (4–10% depending on plan) and risk management protocols to protect your capital. Past performance statistics are fully disclosed.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency (USDT, BTC). Mobile money payments (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo) are available for African clients.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes. All paid plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason within 30 days of purchase, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is available 7 days a week via WhatsApp, Telegram, and email.
